Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about participating in Spark Hack 2025.

What is HackSpire?

HackSpire is a 2-day interschool hackathon where students can collaborate, innovate, and learn while building projects. It's inspired by Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam's 'Wings of Fire' and aims to foster creativity and technical skills.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Bring your laptop, chargers, any development tools you prefer, and a positive attitude! We'll provide meals, snacks, WiFi, and workspace. Don't forget comfortable clothes and maybe a pillow for power naps.

Do I need to have a team before registering?

Not at all! You can register as an individual and we'll help you find teammates during the opening ceremony. We also have team formation activities to help you connect with others who share your interests and skills.

Is there a registration fee?

Nope! Spark Hack is completely free to participate. This includes all meals, snacks, workspace, mentorship, workshops, and swag. Our amazing sponsors make this possible.

What kind of projects can I build?

Anything you can imagine! Web apps, mobile apps, hardware projects, AI/ML solutions, blockchain applications, games, or social impact tools. We'll announce specific challenge tracks at the opening ceremony, but creativity has no limits.

Will there be mentors available?

Absolutely! We have 25+ industry mentors from top tech companies who will be available throughout the event. They can help with technical challenges, business strategy, design decisions, and career advice.
